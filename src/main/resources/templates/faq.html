Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your ride

Reservations
How do I make a reservation? +
Go to Book Now, select a motorcycle and your desired dates, then fill in your personal details in the checkout form. Your reservation will be confirmed immediately.
Can I cancel or modify my reservation? +
To cancel or modify a reservation, please contact us directly via the Contact Us form or by phone at +506 8526-5489. Our team will assist you as soon as possible.
Is there a minimum rental period? +
Yes, the minimum rental period is 1 day. There is no maximum limit — you can rent for as many days as you need.
Requirements
What documents do I need to rent a motorcycle? +
You will need a valid passport and a current driver's license with a motorcycle endorsement from your home country. International licenses are also accepted.
What is the minimum age to rent? +
You must be at least 21 years old to rent a motorcycle with GoRideMoto.
Is prior motorcycle experience required? +
Yes, prior motorcycle riding experience is required. A valid motorcycle license is mandatory. We strongly recommend experience on similar terrain before tackling Costa Rica's roads.
Payment & Insurance
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept cash (USD and CRC colones) and major credit cards. Payment is made at pickup. Online payment is coming soon.
Does the rental include insurance? +
Basic third-party liability insurance is included in all rentals as required by Costa Rican law. Optional comprehensive coverage is available for an additional daily fee.
Is a security deposit required? +
Yes, a refundable security deposit is required at pickup. The amount varies depending on the motorcycle model. It will be returned in full upon safe return of the motorcycle.
Motorcycles & Routes
What types of motorcycles are available? +
We offer adventure and dual-sport motorcycles ideal for Costa Rica's terrain, including models like the Honda CRF300 Rally, Kawasaki KLR650, and Honda CRF250L. Check our Book Now section for current availability.
Can I take the motorcycle to another country? +
No. Motorcycles must remain within Costa Rica at all times. Taking a rental motorcycle outside the country is strictly prohibited and will void the insurance coverage.
What happens if the motorcycle breaks down? +
In case of a breakdown or accident, contact us immediately at +506 8526-5489. We provide roadside assistance and will arrange a replacement motorcycle if needed.
