Rental policies for Go Ride Moto reservations.

Reservations

Reservations are subject to motorcycle availability and confirmation by Go Ride Moto. The customer is responsible for providing accurate contact, date, and pickup information during checkout.

Rental Requirements

  • A valid passport or official identification may be required at pickup.
  • A valid motorcycle license or endorsement is required to rent and operate a motorcycle.
  • Riders must be at least 21 years old unless otherwise approved by Go Ride Moto.

Payments and Deposits

Rental totals shown during booking are estimates based on selected dates and motorcycle rates. Security deposits, optional insurance, accessories, taxes, or additional services may be confirmed at pickup.

Use of Motorcycle

The motorcycle must be used responsibly and within Costa Rica unless Go Ride Moto gives written authorization. The customer is responsible for fines, damage, loss, misuse, and violations during the rental period.

Cancellations and Changes

To modify or cancel a reservation, contact Go Ride Moto as soon as possible. Changes depend on availability and may affect the final rental total.
